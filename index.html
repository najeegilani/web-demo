The System

The Three Pillars of Your Outbound System

01 Technical Infrastructure

The Sending Engine

We don't send from "disposable" or shared accounts. We build on Legitimate Microsoft 365 Infrastructure—the same ecosystem your B2B prospects already live in.

  • The 99-Mailbox Protocol: We configure every domain to support high-density sending (99 mailboxes per domain), allowing for nearly 500 emails per day per domain without triggering bulk-send flags.
  • Ecosystem Trust: By sending from M365, your mail arrives from within the same ecosystem your B2B prospects use (Outlook-to-Outlook), giving you a structural deliverability advantage.
  • The Technical Floor: SPF, DKIM, and DMARC are configured with a strict p=reject policy from day one. Every domain is aged 30–90 days before its first send.
02 Research & Personalization

The AI Operator

Real personalization requires research, and manual research doesn't scale. Our system uses Claude Code to act as a persistent operator inside your data—reading every prospect's website and building row-level context automatically.

  • Deep Website Intelligence: We use Cloudflare Browser Rendering to "read" every prospect's website, distilling their specific service lines and market focus into clean data.
  • The Signal-to-Copy Chain: We turn scraped context into a "Business Signal." Our AI then fills intentional holes in your copy with row-level variables, ensuring every email reads like a human did their homework.
  • Institutional Memory: Every reply and campaign win is captured in a local Obsidian vault, building a compounding "sales brain" that ensures month 12 campaigns are materially better than month 1.
03 Safety & Variation

The Deliverability Guard

To stay in the primary inbox, you must avoid looking like a robot. Every outgoing email runs through a multi-layer system designed to make your volume invisible to spam filters.

  • AI Spam Guard: Every word we generate passes through a seven-layer filter that blocks 350+ "banned words" that statistically trigger junk filters.
  • 2-Phase Spintax Audit: We use a holistic variation system to generate thousands of unique combinations of your email. No two prospects receive an identical "fingerprint," making your volume invisible to filters.
  • Middle-Right Proximity: We strip out "setup language" and position your offer as close to the outcome—meetings and deal flow—as possible.
